## 📝 Query Tips for Best Results

### 1. **Be Specific with Roll Numbers**
   - Use complete roll numbers: "CSE001" or "231FA00001"
   - System recognizes both formats

### 2. **Section Format**
   - Use "SEC-1", "section 5", or just "3" (will be interpreted as SEC-3)
   - System normalizes all formats

### 3. **Subject Names**
   - CN or CNS (Computer Networks)
   - SE (Software Engineering)
   - ADS (Advanced Data Structures)
   - PDC (Parallel and Distributed Computing)

### 4. **Comparison Operators**
   - Less than: "below", "less than", "under", "not more than"
   - Less than or equal: "at most", "less than or equal to", "<="
   - Greater than: "above", "more than", "over", "not less than"
   - Greater than or equal: "at least", "greater than or equal to", ">="

### 5. **Qualifiers**
   - **Low/Weak**: "weak", "poor", "failing", "bad", "struggling", "defaulter"
   - **High/Top**: "top", "best", "excellent", "strong", "outstanding", "topper"
   - **Average**: "average", "moderate", "medium", "normal"

### 6. **Combine Filters**
   - "Top 5 students in SEC-8 with CGPA above 8"
   - "Weak students in PDC from SEC-1 with attendance below 70%"
   - "Students in section 5 with more than 2 backlogs"
