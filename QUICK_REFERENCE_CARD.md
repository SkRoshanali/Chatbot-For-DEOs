## 🔤 Subject Codes

- **SE** = Software Engineering

## 📊 Comparison Operators
- **Less than**: below, less than, under
- **At most**: at most, ≤, less than or equal to
- **Greater than**: above, more than, over
- **At least**: at least, ≥, greater than or equal to

## 🎨 Status Indicators

- 🟡 **Yellow** = Warning (60-75% attendance, 6-8 CGPA)
- 🔴 **Red** = Critical (<75% attendance, <40 external, Fail)

## 💡 Pro Tips
1. **Be specific** - Include section numbers and subject codes
2. **Use natural language** - Ask questions as you would to a person
3. **Combine filters** - "Top 5 students in SEC-8 with CGPA above 8"
4. **Export reports** - Use CSV, Excel, or PDF export buttons
5. **Clear cache** - Use Ctrl+Shift+R if page doesn't load
